PHD Courses

Our PHDs lead the effort to extend the boundaries of knowledge through quality and industry relevant research.

The Department of Computer Science is dedicated to fostering a vibrant learning community for students by organizing a series of enriching events. These events cater to various interests and include workshops, hackathons, guest lectures, and networking sessions. Through these initiatives, students gain valuable opportunities to enhance their technical skills, explore emerging trends in the field, and engage with industry professionals. The events promote collaboration, innovation, and knowledge sharing among students, encouraging them to push the boundaries of computer science. Additionally, these gatherings facilitate networking, enabling students to establish connections with peers, faculty members, and potential employers. The department's commitment to hosting such events cultivates a dynamic and inclusive environment that fuels students' passion for computer science and prepares them for success in their future careers.
